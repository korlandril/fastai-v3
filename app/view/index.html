Military Camo Classifier

This model was trained on various images featuring five different military camouflage designs in various lighting conditions and foci: ABU (United States Air Force), CADPAT (Canadian Armed Forces), M90 (Swedish Armed Forces), and M75 (Norwegian Armed Forces).

The model is supposed to take in an image and make a high probability decision of what camo pattern that image features. Feel free to try it out with anything on Google Images or photos you happen to have! Interested in how this was built? Blog post (hopefully) coming soon!
